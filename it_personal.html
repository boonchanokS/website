Our Vision, Missions and Values

Vision

SRT Vision

SRT is the subsidiary of Sumitomo Group which strives to devote to the society and contribute to creating the actual, sustainable society through implementation of social responsibility and environmental activities by adhering to the principle of GENKI (Green, Ecology, Next, Kindness, and Integrity). For this reason, the Company has earned trust from the society.


All the employees would make concerted effort to achieve these goals in order to contribute to creating sustainable society.


Mr. Kenji Saito

President of Sumitomo Rubber (Thailand) Co., Ltd.
